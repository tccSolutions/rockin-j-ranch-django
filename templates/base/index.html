About The Ranch

Rocking-J-Ranch was founded over 20 years ago in North Geogia with the mindset of rehibillitating and rehoming wild mustangs. The owern's, David and Karen Jones, have been animal lovers from their first breath. Together they saw a need for wild mustangs to be homed. They realized that before these great animals can be homed they would need to be genteled. Thus, Rocking-J-Ranch was created. Since its founding, the ranch has trained and re-homed numerous mustangs. Please visit the barn if you are interested in finding out more about the mustangs on the property.

Training Techniques

It has been proven time and time again that the old way of "breaking" horses is broken. At the Rockin-J-Ranch, the first priority is always the animals health and safety. We use the same techniques as many other world-famous trainers, such as Clinton Anderson and Ray Hunt. These techniques focus heavily on ground-work and developing a mutual respect between horse, trainer, and rider.
